    SAP—Design Program Manager Intern

    06/2021 — 10/2021

    At SAP, I managed a wide scope of work for over 90 members in a global UX Organization. To sum it up, Design Program Management is a mix of:

  • 40% Program Management
  • 40% Project Management
  • 20% Product Management

    • Design program management is a specialized form of program management that focuses on managing the design and development of products or services. Often times, this takes on the form of large program initiatives, but in addition to regular project management activities which support the regular delivery intervals of UX design deliveries. This would come in the form of maintaining user stories and roadmaps by working with the design team, and ensuring project management practices are upheld.

    Essentially, Design Program Managers handle program initiatives in addition to the project management activities for the teams they are supporting, similar to a Technical Program Manager.

    Some notable highlights include:

  • Delivered a UX Design Template, which resulted in an average of a 15% increase in UX Design Project Efficiency
  • Worked cross-functionally with Engineering and Product Management to streamline inefficiencies
  • Established new Agile Project Management practices spanning across the entire organization
