SAP — Optimizing Project Management for UX Design

Drawing upon my expertise in UX Design processes gained during my initial project at SAP, I played a pivotal role in spearheading a new initiative aimed at enhancing Project Management efficiency for a UX organization. This initiative was implemented for 90+ UX Designers within a well-established UX Design organization, and I collaborated closely with a seasoned Senior PM throughout the process.

In the last 5 years, the SAP BTP software development organization has witnessed rapid expansion, resulting in a substantial increase in the number of UX design projects and teams. While the overall development organization has adopted the SAFe project management framework, the Design organization's project management practices have remained static. This misalignment between our organization's growth and the absence of updated project management processes has given rise to several critical challenges, including inefficiencies in project execution, suboptimal resource allocation, breakdowns in communication, and difficulties in prioritizing Design backlog items.

    The Project

    The VAS Community was designed to serve as an all-inclusive help website where customers can access articles, engage in discussions with other farmers on forums, and seek customer support through live chats. As time passed, the objective was to encourage customers to use this service more frequently than contacting the customer support, as it proved to be a more cost-effective option.

    Daniel's Role:

  • Daniel supported the project management within a 2 week sprint environment and facilitated team discussions for stand-up meetings (3x/week), sprint reviews, and retrospectives.
  • Responsible for forecasting Sprint iterations with the development team and stakeholders. He managed the queue of deliverables, adjusting priorities according to the needs of the business on the sprint board within the project management software, Jira.
  • Worked with the Product Owner to investigate the list of Jira tickets within the backlog
  • Crafted the Launch and engagement strategy project to invite the existing customer base onto the customer forum/support site. This consisted of working closely with Marketing and managing deliverables, personally creating and managing the A/B test invitation email campaigns, and executed upon an engagement strategy to keep users engaged for the first 2 months of the launch.
